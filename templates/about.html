Hello!

My name is Hardi Tri Nugroho , a final-year Informatics student with a keen interest in the intersection of machine learning and audio processing.

I am currently working on my thesis title "Implementation of MFCC and CNN in Gender Voice Recognition." This project combines the power of Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC) for feature extraction and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for classification, aiming to develop an efficient and accurate system for identifying gender from voice recordings.
I am passionate about exploring the endless possibilities in the field of artificial intelligence and am excited to contribute to advancements in voice recognition technology.
